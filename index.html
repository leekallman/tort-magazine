Tort Magazine

Tort Magazine is an independent magazine about contoversial contemporary issues written by a remote editorial team based in Stockholm and London.

We explore present societal issues through honest and in-depth conversations with individuals directly or indirectly questioning norms and committed to changing the way we live our lives.

In honest and in-depth portraits we get to know individuals who want to improve our society through their legal, cultural, academical, political, personal (or an intersection of these) engagements. Who are they and what drives them? The conversations are close and insightful, delivered in a high quality printed-paper. In addition to the interviews, contributors writing what is on top of their mind, such as fiction and poetry, tech, culture and criticism, will represent Tort. The people behind TORT do not have the same background, however we share the interest in societal matters, humans, text and photography. In the spirit of this, TORT was created by curiosity and with a dedication to inspire and engage. We therefore immerse ourselves in this project– not only must the portrayed people be carefully selected; our questions must be sharp, our research thoroughly, the text well written and the pictures appealing. We want to move and trigger the reader with our magazine.

The content of this inaugural issue has been created together with five writers and an editor. An illustrator, a photographer, a graphic designer, a web developer, and an art director have all exercised their command on the graphic and visual displays. During evenings, nights, weekdays, and weekends leading up to this point, we’ve all been working pro bono to bring forth what finally is Tort.

Tort Magazine is non-partisan and religiously independent.

We are looking for volunteers to photograph, write, illustrate and distribute so please get in touch: contact@tortmagazine.com
